INDOOR SPORTS

Explore Indoor Sports

Table Tennis

Table tennis is a fast-paced indoor sport where two or four players use paddles to hit a lightweight ball back and forth across a table. The objective is to score points by making the ball land in the opponent's half of the table without them returning it.

Boxing

A combat sport where two fighters compete using punches within a ring, wearing gloves. The objective is to outscore the opponent or achieve a knockout. Matches are divided into timed rounds, and fighters must use skill, strategy, and endurance while adhering to strict rules.

Chess

A two-player strategy game played on a square board with 64 squares. Each player controls 16 pieces, including a king, queen, rooks, bishops, knights, and pawns. The goal is to checkmate the opponent's king.

Ludo

A classic board game where 2 to 4 players race to move their pieces from start to finish based on dice rolls. The game involves strategy and luck, and players can block each other's tokens to prevent progress.

Carrom

A popular indoor game where players flick small discs on a board, trying to sink them into the corner pockets. The objective is to sink your pieces first, while also knocking out your opponent's pieces.

Snooker

A cue sport played on a large table covered with green cloth, using a cue stick to pot balls in the correct sequence. It requires precision, skill, and strategy.
