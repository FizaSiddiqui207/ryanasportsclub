Boxing

Boxing Rules and Timing Details

About Boxing: Boxing is a combat sport where two players compete to land punches while following the rules of the game.

Rules:

  • A match consists of a specified number of 3-minute rounds.
  • Punches must land on the front or sides of the opponent above the belt.
  • Fouls include hitting below the belt, headbutting, or using elbows.
  • Knockouts and technical knockouts decide the match if a boxer cannot continue.
  • The winner is determined by points, knockouts, or the referee's decision.

Timings:

  • Weekdays: 6 PM - 9 PM
  • Weekends: 5 PM - 9 PM
